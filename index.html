What we do

We match social enterprises with Impact investors, Companies Corporate Social Responsibility(CSR) funds based on several factors such as geography, industry, stage, cash flow, exit potential etc.

Startup/ Investors registration

Startups who are looking for funding get registered here alongside with Investors, past registration we ask to fill general information, so that we run our recommendation based on that.

Match according to preferences

Our match making and recommendation algorithms have the best accuracy with 90% success rate. We match and recommend for both the investors and startups based on thier interests and preferences to avoid conflicts.

Raise Capital

Once both startup and investor or company accept the match which we provide, then that's it congratulations.
